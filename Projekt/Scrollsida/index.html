HARRY POTTER
CHARACTERS

THE GOLDEN TRIO

THE GOLDEN TRIO REFERS TO THE THREE MAIN CHARACTERS IN THE HARRY POTTER SERIES WRITTEN BY J.K. ROWLING. THESE THREE CHARACTERS, HARRY POTTER, HERMIONE GRANGER, AND RON WEASLEY, FORM A CLOSE-KNIT FRIENDSHIP THROUGHOUT THEIR YEARS AT HOGWARTS SCHOOL OF WITCHCRAFT AND WIZARDRY.

HARRY POTTER

HARRY JAMES POTTER IS THE PROTAGONIST OF THE SERIES. HE IS KNOWN FOR HIS DISTINCTIVE LIGHTNING-SHAPED SCAR ON HIS FOREHEAD, A RESULT OF A FAILED CURSE FROM THE DARK WIZARD LORD VOLDEMORT. HARRY IS COURAGEOUS, LOYAL, AND DETERMINED TO STAND UP AGAINST THE FORCES OF EVIL.

HERMIONE GRANGER

HERMIONE JEAN GRANGER IS THE INTELLIGENT AND RESOURCEFUL MEMBER OF THE TRIO. SHE IS KNOWN FOR HER ACADEMIC PROWESS AND QUICK THINKING IN DIFFICULT SITUATIONS. HERMIONE IS MUGGLE-BORN, MEANING SHE WAS BORN TO NON-MAGICAL PARENTS, BUT SHE EXCELS IN HER MAGICAL STUDIES AND BECOMES AN INVALUABLE ASSET TO THE GROUP.

RON WEASLEY

RONALD BILIUS "RON" WEASLEY IS THE LOYAL AND HUMOROUS FRIEND OF HARRY AND HERMIONE. HE COMES FROM A LARGE WIZARDING FAMILY AND IS OFTEN RECOGNIZED BY HIS FIERY RED HAIR. DESPITE FACING INSECURITIES, RON PROVES TO BE A BRAVE COMPANION, ALWAYS READY TO SUPPORT HIS FRIENDS IN THE FACE OF DANGER.

THE PROFESSORS

HOGWARTS SCHOOL OF WITCHCRAFT AND WIZARDRY IS HOME TO A VARIETY OF SKILLED AND DIVERSE PROFESSORS. THESE EDUCATORS PLAY A CRUCIAL ROLE IN SHAPING THE MAGICAL EDUCATION OF YOUNG WITCHES AND WIZARDS.

ALBUS DUMBLEDORE - HEADMASTER

PROFESSOR ALBUS DUMBLEDORE SERVES AS THE WISE AND BELOVED HEADMASTER OF HOGWARTS. HIS LEADERSHIP GUIDES THE SCHOOL THROUGH TOUGH TIMES, AND HIS LEGENDARY STATUS MAKES HIM A SOURCE OF INSPIRATION FOR STUDENTS AND FACULTY ALIKE.

MINERVA MCGONAGALL - TRANSFIGURATION

PROFESSOR MINERVA MCGONAGALL HEADS THE TRANSFIGURATION DEPARTMENT. SHE IS KNOWN FOR HER STRICT YET FAIR TEACHING STYLE. MCGONAGALL IS ALSO THE HEAD OF GRYFFINDOR HOUSE AND TRANSFORMS INTO A CAT AT WILL.

SEVERUS SNAPE - POTIONS

PROFESSOR SEVERUS SNAPE TEACHES POTIONS AT HOGWARTS. HIS STERN DEMEANOR HIDES A COMPLEX CHARACTER, AND HIS EXPERTISE IN THE DARK ARTS MAKES HIM A KEY PLAYER IN THE BATTLE AGAINST THE DARK LORD.

THESE PROFESSORS, ALONG WITH MANY OTHERS, CONTRIBUTE TO THE RICH EDUCATIONAL EXPERIENCE AT HOGWARTS, EACH BRINGING THEIR UNIQUE EXPERTISE AND PASSION TO THE MAGICAL WORLD OF WIZARDRY.

THE DEATH EATERS

THE WIZARDING WORLD IS NOT WITHOUT ITS SHARE OF FORMIDABLE VILLAINS. THESE DARK CHARACTERS PLAY CRUCIAL ROLES IN THE CONFLICT THAT UNFOLDS THROUGHOUT THE HARRY POTTER SERIES.

LORD VOLDEMORT

LORD VOLDEMORT, BORN TOM MARVOLO RIDDLE, IS THE PRIMARY ANTAGONIST OF THE SERIES. HE SEEKS TO CONQUER THE WIZARDING WORLD, IMPOSING HIS PURE-BLOOD IDEOLOGY. HIS QUEST FOR IMMORTALITY AND POWER LEADS TO THE RISE OF THE DEATH EATERS, A GROUP DEDICATED TO SERVING HIM.

BELLATRIX LESTRANGE

BELLATRIX LESTRANGE IS A DEVOTED DEATH EATER KNOWN FOR HER FANATIC LOYALTY TO VOLDEMORT. HER SKILLS IN DARK MAGIC AND HER CRUELTY MAKE HER A FORMIDABLE ADVERSARY. BELLATRIX IS RESPONSIBLE FOR SEVERAL ACTS OF VIOLENCE AND TRAGEDY THROUGHOUT THE SERIES.

DRACO MALFOY

DRACO MALFOY, THOUGH INITIALLY PORTRAYED AS A SCHOOL RIVAL TO HARRY, IS DRAWN INTO THE DEATH EATER CIRCLE DUE TO FAMILY EXPECTATIONS. DESPITE HIS UPBRINGING, DRACO STRUGGLES WITH THE MORALITY OF DARK DEEDS AND UNDERGOES PERSONAL GROWTH AS THE SERIES PROGRESSES.

THESE VILLAINS, AMONG OTHERS, CONTRIBUTE TO THE TENSION AND DRAMA IN THE WIZARDING WORLD, CREATING A FORMIDABLE OPPOSITION FOR THE PROTAGONISTS AND ADDING DEPTH TO THE OVERARCHING STORY OF HARRY POTTER.

LOCATIONS

HOGWARTS

HOGWARTS IS A MAGICAL PLACE OF LEARNING, NESTLED AMIDST THE SCENIC HIGHLANDS OF SCOTLAND. IT STANDS AS THE PREMIER INSTITUTION FOR YOUNG WITCHES AND WIZARDS TO HONE THEIR MAGICAL ABILITIES AND EMBARK ON EPIC ADVENTURES.

HOUSE SYSTEM

HOGWARTS IS DIVIDED INTO FOUR HOUSES: GRYFFINDOR, HUFFLEPUFF, RAVENCLAW, AND SLYTHERIN. EACH HOUSE HAS ITS UNIQUE TRAITS, VALUES, AND DORMITORIES. STUDENTS ARE SORTED INTO HOUSES UPON THEIR ARRIVAL, FOSTERING A STRONG SENSE OF COMMUNITY AND COMPETITION.

CLASSES AND PROFESSORS

AT HOGWARTS, STUDENTS ATTEND CLASSES TAUGHT BY EXPERIENCED PROFESSORS. SUBJECTS INCLUDE POTIONS, TRANSFIGURATION, CHARMS, DEFENSE AGAINST THE DARK ARTS, AND MANY MORE. THE DEDICATED FACULTY ENSURES THAT STUDENTS RECEIVE A WELL-ROUNDED MAGICAL EDUCATION.

THE GREAT HALL

THE MAGNIFICENT GREAT HALL SERVES AS THE HEART OF HOGWARTS. STUDENTS GATHER HERE FOR MEALS, HOUSE POINTS ARE AWARDED, AND IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENTS ARE MADE. THE CEILING OF THE GREAT HALL REFLECTS THE CURRENT WEATHER OUTSIDE.

HOGWARTS, WITH ITS TOWERS, SECRET PASSAGES, AND ENCHANTED SURROUNDINGS, PROVIDES AN ENCHANTING BACKDROP FOR THE UNFORGETTABLE JOURNEY OF EVERY WITCH AND WIZARD WHO CALLS IT HOME.

DIAGON ALLEY

DIAGON ALLEY IS A MAGICAL SHOPPING DISTRICT HIDDEN FROM MUGGLES, ACCESSIBLE THROUGH THE LEAKY CAULDRON. IT SERVES AS THE PRIMARY DESTINATION FOR WIZARDS AND WITCHES TO ACQUIRE ALL THEIR MAGICAL SUPPLIES AND ENCHANTING NECESSITIES.

SHOPS AND ESTABLISHMENTS

DIAGON ALLEY BOASTS A WIDE ARRAY OF SHOPS, EACH SPECIALIZING IN DIFFERENT MAGICAL GOODS. FROM OLLIVANDER'S WAND SHOP, WHERE WIZARDS FIND THEIR PERFECT WANDS, TO FLOURISH AND BLOTTS, A BOOKSTORE STOCKED WITH MAGICAL TEXTS, EVERY NEED OF A WITCH OR WIZARD CAN BE MET.

GRINGOTTS BANK

GRINGOTTS, THE WIZARDING BANK, IS LOCATED IN DIAGON ALLEY. PROTECTED BY GRIPHOOK THE GOBLIN AND HIS FELLOW GOBLINS, GRINGOTTS IS KNOWN FOR ITS HIGH-SECURITY VAULTS THAT HOUSE WIZARDS' MOST VALUABLE POSSESSIONS AND, OF COURSE, THE MAGICAL CURRENCY - GALLEONS, SICKLES, AND KNUCKLES.

THE LEAKY CAULDRON

DIAGON ALLEY'S ENTRANCE FOR WIZARDS IS THROUGH THE LEAKY CAULDRON, A WIZARDING PUB. BEHIND THE BRICK WALL ACCESSED THROUGH THE PUB LIES THE ENCHANTING WORLD OF DIAGON ALLEY.

DIAGON ALLEY IS A HUB OF MAGICAL COMMERCE AND COMMUNITY, WHERE WIZARDS AND WITCHES EMBARK ON THEIR MAGICAL JOURNEYS BY OBTAINING EVERYTHING THEY NEED TO NAVIGATE THE WIZARDING WORLD.

GAMES

HOGWARTS LEGACY

"HOGWARTS LEGACY" IS AN IMMERSIVE ACTION ROLE-PLAYING GAME SET IN THE MAGICAL UNIVERSE CREATED BY J.K. ROWLING. DEVELOPED BY PORTKEY GAMES, THIS GAME PROMISES AN ENCHANTING JOURNEY INTO THE WIZARDING WORLD, ALLOWING PLAYERS TO EXPERIENCE THE MAGIC AND MYSTERIES OF HOGWARTS SCHOOL OF WITCHCRAFT AND WIZARDRY.

GAMEPLAY AND EXPLORATION

PLAYERS STEP INTO THE SHOES OF A STUDENT AT HOGWARTS, CREATING THEIR OWN MAGICAL CHARACTER AND EMBARKING ON A UNIQUE JOURNEY. THE GAME IS SET IN THE 1800S, LONG BEFORE THE EVENTS OF THE HARRY POTTER BOOK SERIES, PROVIDING A FRESH PERSPECTIVE ON THE WIZARDING WORLD. THE OPEN-WORLD FORMAT ALLOWS FOR EXPLORATION OF ICONIC LOCATIONS, ENCOUNTERS WITH MAGICAL CREATURES, AND THE DISCOVERY OF HIDDEN SECRETS.

MAGIC AND SPELLCASTING

"HOGWARTS LEGACY" PLACES A STRONG EMPHASIS ON MAGIC AND SPELLCASTING. PLAYERS CAN ATTEND CLASSES TO LEARN AND MASTER A VARIETY OF SPELLS, POTIONS, AND MAGICAL ABILITIES. THE DYNAMIC SPELL SYSTEM AIMS TO OFFER A CAPTIVATING AND INTERACTIVE EXPERIENCE, ALLOWING PLAYERS TO UNLEASH THEIR MAGICAL PROWESS IN DUELS, QUESTS, AND CHALLENGES.

CREATURES AND QUESTS

THE GAME FEATURES A RICH NARRATIVE WITH A DIVERSE CAST OF CHARACTERS, MAGICAL CREATURES, AND ENGAGING QUESTS. AS PLAYERS PROGRESS THROUGH THE STORY, THEY WILL FACE MORAL CHOICES THAT IMPACT THE OUTCOME OF THEIR CHARACTER'S JOURNEY, SHAPING THE WIZARDING WORLD AROUND THEM.

"HOGWARTS LEGACY" PROMISES TO BE A CAPTIVATING AND IMMERSIVE EXPERIENCE, INVITING PLAYERS TO LIVE OUT THEIR MAGICAL FANTASIES IN A BEAUTIFULLY REALIZED WIZARDING WORLD.

HOGWARTS MYSTERY

"HARRY POTTER: HOGWARTS MYSTERY" INVITES PLAYERS TO EXPERIENCE THE ENCHANTING WORLD OF MAGIC AND MYSTERY, SET IN THE ICONIC HOGWARTS SCHOOL OF WITCHCRAFT AND WIZARDRY. DEVELOPED BY JAM CITY, THIS MOBILE GAME ALLOWS YOU TO CREATE YOUR OWN WIZARD OR WITCH CHARACTER AND EMBARK ON A CAPTIVATING ADVENTURE.

CREATE YOUR CHARACTER

BEFORE YOU STEP THROUGH THE MAGICAL DOORS OF HOGWARTS, YOU HAVE THE OPPORTUNITY TO CREATE YOUR OWN CHARACTER. CHOOSE YOUR HOUSE, CUSTOMIZE YOUR APPEARANCE, AND PREPARE TO IMMERSE YOURSELF IN THE WORLD OF SPELLS AND POTIONS.

ATTEND CLASSES AND LEARN MAGIC

AS A STUDENT AT HOGWARTS, ATTENDING CLASSES IS A FUNDAMENTAL PART OF YOUR MAGICAL EDUCATION. LEARN SPELLS, BREW POTIONS, AND UNLOCK THE SECRETS OF THE WIZARDING WORLD. ENGAGE WITH ICONIC PROFESSORS, INCLUDING PROFESSOR SNAPE AND PROFESSOR MCGONAGALL, AS YOU PROGRESS THROUGH YOUR YEARS AT HOGWARTS.

FORM FRIENDSHIPS AND UNCOVER MYSTERIES

FORGE FRIENDSHIPS WITH FELLOW STUDENTS LIKE ROWAN, BEN, AND MERULA, AND NAVIGATE THE CHALLENGES OF MAGICAL SCHOOL LIFE. AS YOU UNRAVEL THE MYSTERIES WITHIN HOGWARTS, MAKE DECISIONS THAT WILL INFLUENCE YOUR CHARACTER'S STORY AND SHAPE THE COURSE OF YOUR MAGICAL JOURNEY.

EMBARK ON A MAGICAL JOURNEY, ATTEND HOGWARTS, AND UNCOVER THE MYSTERIES THAT LIE WITHIN THE WALLS OF THE WORLD'S MOST FAMOUS SCHOOL OF MAGIC. YOUR ADVENTURE IN THE WIZARDING WORLD AWAITS!
